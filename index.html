About Versus A Bear

Versus a Bear (VaB) is a monthly podcast where 3 idiots debate for 3 rounds which fictional, or real, characters would win in a fight. And whether or not the victor could go toe-to-toe with a bear.

Kevin James from "Here Comes The Boom" vs. a Full Grown Grizzly

The Rounds

  1. Head-to-Head Round: Each Host states their case for their individual characters and why they think they should get the honor of fighting a bear. The Moderator chooses two hosts/champions to move on to the next round.
  2. Reinforcement Round: The surviving hosts can each bring in 1 character from within the universe of their champion to aid in the fight. At the end of the round the Moderator makes their decision. Winner get's to move on to the final round.
  3. Versus A Bear: The surviving champion get's a chance to prove their worth! "The Wheel of Bear Necessities" is spun, giving the Champion, AND the Bear some sort of aid (or hinderance) and the Host pleads their case. At the end only one can stand victorious, as chosen of course by the moderator.
